Chicken Lemon Lentil

  • 3 carrots
  • 1 celery
  • 2 onions
  • 2 lbs. diced Chicken
  • Zest of 1 Lemon
  • 4 cups of lentils
  • 4 litres of Chicken Stock
  • Oregano, thyme, basil to taste
  • salt, to taste
  1. brown chicken with 1/8th cup of oil
  2. Sweat mirepoix
  3. add stock and seasonings and let boil
  4. add lentils and turn down heat to simmer
  5. simmer until Lentils are cooked and tender
